[**5 Best LLMs You Can Use for Free**](https://www.whytryai.com/p/best-free-llms)

* **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** (Anthropic)

  * It’s [the #1 LLM on ](https://x.com/alexandr_wang/status/1807828398523249099)[*Instruction Following*](https://x.com/alexandr_wang/status/1807828398523249099)[ and ](https://x.com/alexandr_wang/status/1807828398523249099)[*Coding*](https://x.com/alexandr_wang/status/1807828398523249099) as measured by the [independent SEAL Leaderboard](https://www.whytryai.com/i/145021019/ai-random).

  * **Standout features:** Best scores on most LLM benchmarks, interactive “Artifacts” window, great at summarizing long docs.

  * **Where to try:** [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai/)

* **Gemini 1.5 Pro** (Google)

  * The Google AI Studio has some downsides:

    * The interface is not quite as intuitive (but isn’t too difficult to get used to).

    * The models inside it can’t browse the Internet directly.

  * **Where to try:** [aistudio.google.com](https://aistudio.google.com/)**** or [notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com/)

* **GPT-4o** (OpenAI)

  * The latest model from OpenAI is still hanging on to its #1 rank on the LMSYS Chatbot Arena.

  * Even as a free ChatGPT user, you get to access GPT-4o, [Code Interpreter / Advanced Data Analysis](https://www.whytryai.com/p/chatgpt-code-interpreter-getting-started), and the ability to browse the web, upload files, and use other people’s [Custom GPTs](https://www.whytryai.com/p/build-gpts).

* **Llama 3 70B Instruct** (Meta)

  * **Where to try:** [Meta.ai](https://www.meta.ai/), one of the 100+ spaces on [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/meta-llama), or [GitHub](https://github.com/huggingface/blog/blob/main/llama3.md).

* **Pi / Inflection 2.5** (Inflection AI)

  * Pi is perhaps the most emotionally intelligent chatbot around. It has an encouraging, compassionate, supportive tone.&#x20;

  * Pi also supports voice conversations and is one of the most natural voice chat experiences I’ve had with an LLM

  * **Where to try:** [Pi.ai](https://pi.ai/talk)
